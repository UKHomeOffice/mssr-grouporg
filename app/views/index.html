Modern slavery statement registry

In-scope feature

Functionalities covered in this prototype:

New statement submission:

  • Organisations could declare their scope status using the column in the table
  • Organisations could declare their scope status wihtin the journey (if they have missed doing it from the table)
  • Organisations could only declare their scope status as NO and do nothing to submit the statement

Editing statements:

  • Organisations could change their scope status from Yes to No / No to Yes / Reasons for No
  • Organisations coming back to submit the statement voluntarily who has declared their scope status as NO

Viewing statements:

  • Organisations are in scope to publish the statement
  • Organisations are out of scope, but submitted the statement voluntarily
  • Organisations are out of scope, so didn't submitted the statement
